Introduction

Xpat Computer Solutions was registered in 1993 with the sole purpose of developing software for local and later international markets. This was the result of observations we made that showed that while many institutions had the capability to buy expensive and fast computers, they lacked tools that can help them to significantly enhance their productivity using the available technology.
